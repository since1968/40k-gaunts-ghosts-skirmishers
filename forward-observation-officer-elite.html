FOCUSED BOMBARDMENT

Astra Militarum Stratagem

At the precise moment, the Forward Observation Officer calls every gun at their disposal to fire on a single point.

Use this Stratagem in your Shooting phase, when a Forward Observation Officer unit from your army is chosen to shoot with. Until the end of that phase, the artillery barrage that unit is equipped with has a Type characteristic of Heavy 6.
